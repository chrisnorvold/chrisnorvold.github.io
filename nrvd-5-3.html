The Founder

Norvold
Role Filmmaker & Founder
Company NRVD LLC
Discipline Storytelling & Strategy
Roots Endurance Sports & Film

I've spent most of my career behind a camera in some pretty unglamorous places — chasing cyclists through mud, freezing on mountain bike trails at 5am, trying to make a 10-second clip feel like a full story. That's where NRVD started. Not in a boardroom.

"I kept seeing the same gaps over and over — in how creatives get hired, how stories get told, how talent gets treated. So I started building the things I wished existed."

Pursue came first — a creative agency doing real work for real athletes and brands. The kind of stuff where the story actually matters. From there, Crafted was born out of pure frustration with how hard it is to find good creative work and good creative people in the same place.

None of this was planned on a whiteboard. It grew from doing the work, staying close to the people doing it, and refusing to build something I wouldn't personally use.
